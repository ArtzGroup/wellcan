Welcome to WellCan

Well-being resources and live virtual counselling for Canadians during the COVID-19 pandemic.


The COVID-19 pandemic presents complex and unprecedented challenges, impacting all parts of Canadian life: how we socialize, how we work, how we get our essentials, and how we maintain our overall health and well-being. And while ensuring we take all recommended preventative measures to protect ourselves, our hospitals and healthcare workers, it is important for all Canadians to maintain good mental, physical, social and financial health in the midst of this pandemic. WellCan can help.

What is WellCan?

WellCan offers free well-being resources to help Canadians develop coping strategies and build resilience and access to free, confidential counselling to help deal with uncertainty, mental health and substance abuse concerns during the COVID-19 pandemic.

Start with an assessment

Assessments can help identify resources that will be most useful based on your concerns and circumstances. The assessments guide you through a series of questions and provide a score for anxiety, depression, and substance abuse. After completing the assessment, you will receive a recommendation to explore specific resources on WellCan, or to contact us for support.


Explore available resources

WellCan’s Explore section offers a number of useful resources available 24/7 to help you manage your well-being throughout the COVID-19 pandemic, including:

  • Toolkits to adapt to remote work
  • Tips on effective physical distancing and using technology to combat loneliness in isolation/quarantine
  • Articles about managing feelings of sadness, anxiety, stress and more

Get support when you need it

Your assessment may recommend you contact us for support right away. You have access to two types of counselling, available whether or not you take an assessment to start:

  • Telephone counselling
  • Online chat counselling

The WellCan App: convenient, easy, secure

  • Search WellCan in the Google Play or Apple App Stores, and select ‘Install’ / ‘Get’
  • Allow push notifications so you’re up to date on new resources and receive counsellor messages (text-based counselling only)
  • Browse COVID-19 health and well-being resources in the app
  • Reach qualified counsellors for telephone or online chat counselling
