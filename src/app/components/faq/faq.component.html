FAQs

What is WellCan?

WellCan offers free resources to help develop coping strategies and build resilience and access to free, confidential counselling to help deal with uncertainty during the COVID-19 pandemic.

You can start with an assessment to rate your risk factors for things like anxiety, depression and substance abuse, which can help guide you toward the support you need: either direction to specific resources available on WellCan, counselling, or both.

Am I eligible to use this?

All Canadian citizens and residents are eligible and encouraged to use WellCan.

Is there a cost for using this service?

There is no cost to use any of the resources or counselling services available through WellCan.

Is my personal information confidential?

Any personal-identifiable, health-related or other information shared in your counselling session are private and confidential. WellCan is subject to and compliant with all Canadian privacy laws.

What resources are available through WellCan?

There are a number of toolkits, articles, tips and infographics available in the Explore resources section, which cover a range of topics including (but not limited to):

  • Physical distancing, handwashing and hygiene
  • Remote working/telecommuting
  • Using technology to combat loneliness in isolation/quarantine
  • Boosting resilience and coping strategies in uncertain times
  • How to talk to your children about COVID-19
When can I access counselling?

Both telephone and online chat counselling are available between the hours of 10:00 a.m. – 12:00 a.m., 7 days a week, in all time zones across Canada.

Who provides counselling services for WellCan?

Both the resources in WellCan’s Explore section and counselling services are provided by Morneau Shepell, through their LifeWorks well-being business.

How many counselling sessions are available to me?

Both counselling options are available on demand, as needed, between the hours of 10:00 a.m. – 12:00 a.m., 7 days a week, in all time zones across Canada. Telephone counselling can be a one-time service or ongoing with the same counsellor. Online chat counselling will match you with a new counsellor each time and does not provide ongoing sessions with the same counsellor.
