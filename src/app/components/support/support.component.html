Alberta

Health Link

A free, round-the-clock, telephone advice and health information service. Highly experienced Registered Nurses can provide advice and information about:

  • Health concerns or symptoms
  • Treating conditions such as colds and flu, fever, ear aches, rashes, and; many other conditions
  • The most appropriate health care option, if you need further follow up for your condition
  • Family Doctors located in Alberta

Phone Numbers

  • Toll-free: 1-866-408-5465
  • Edmonton: 780-408-5465
  • Calgary: 403-943-5465

British Columbia

Health Link BC

HealthLink BC is a service that provides free, non-emergency health information. You can get help and information any time of the day or night, every day of the year. Call HealthLink BC to learn about health topics, check your symptoms, and find the services you need. They can help you or your family member in more than 130 languages.

Phone Numbers

  • 888-COVID19
  • 8-1-1

Manitoba

Health Links

In addition to getting answers to your questions, you can ask for advice and obtain help to find health care resources in your community.

New Bruinswick

Tele-Care

Tele-Care is a free, confidential, health advice and information line. Dial 811 for access to bilingual, registered nurses, 24 hours a day, seven days a week. In an emergency, call 911 or visit the local emergency department.

Phone Numbers

  • Toll-free: 8-1-1
  • Emergencies: 9-1-1

Nova Scotia

Health Link

Call 8-1-1a to talk to a registered nurse around the clock, seven days a week. Nurses can also provide information about services provided in your area, or general information about your health care concerns.

811 can help you with advice on everything from bee stings, frost bite, to coughs, and flu. Top five most common reasons for calling 811:

  • Abdominal pain / discomfort
  • Chest pain / discomfort
  • Children’s colds & coughs
  • Adult colds & coughs
  • Adult nausea & vomiting

Phone Numbers

  • Toll-free: 8-1-1

Ontario

Telehealth

Free telephonic access to a Registered Nurse 24 Hours a day, 7 days a Week.

Telehealth Ontario is a free, confidential telephone service offering general health advice or information from a Registered Nurse who can assess symptoms and help you decide whether to care for yourself, make an appointment with your doctor, go to a clinic, contact a community service or go to a hospital emergency room.

Phone Numbers

  • Toll-free: 1-866-797-0000

Prince Edward Island

Telehealth

A registered nurse is available 24 hours a day to answer your health questions over the phone and will:

  • help you determine whether you require emergency or non-urgent medical attention
  • provide current, reliable information related to your health issue
  • offer helpful guidance about health services available in the province

Phone Numbers

  • Toll-free: 8-1-1

Yukon

Yokun HealthLine

The Yukon HealthLine is a free, confidential 24-hour service available to anyone in the Yukon. Yukon HealthLine is staffed by registered nurses who can answer your health questions or direct you to someone in your community who can assist you.
